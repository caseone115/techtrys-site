FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What Power BI licensing do I need? ▾

It depends on your scale and requirements. Pro licenses ($14 USD/user/month) are suitable for most report consumers. Premium Per User ($24 USD/user/month) adds AI features and larger data capacity. For enterprise-wide deployment with dedicated capacity, Premium Fabric SKUs starting at ~$5,000 USD/month provide the best performance.

What is DAX and why does it matter? ▾

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) is the formula language Power BI uses for calculations. Poorly written DAX is the leading cause of slow reports and incorrect numbers. We follow strict DAX patterns — proper use of CALCULATE, filter contexts, and star-schema modelling — that ensure your measures are accurate, performant, and maintainable.

How do I improve slow Power BI reports? ▾

Slow reports are almost always caused by poor data modelling, not Power BI itself. The fix involves moving transformations upstream to your data warehouse, building star-schema models, optimising DAX measures, and reducing visual complexity. We typically see 5-10x performance improvements after a proper optimisation engagement.

Can Power BI connect to my existing data sources? ▾

Power BI supports 150+ built-in connectors including Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, SQL Server, SharePoint, Excel, and REST APIs. For custom or legacy systems, we build data pipelines using Power Query or Azure Data Factory to stage and transform your data before loading it into Power BI for optimal performance.

Can I embed Power BI reports into my own application? ▾

Yes — Power BI Embedded allows you to integrate interactive reports and dashboards directly into your own web applications and portals. We handle the authentication, licensing, and API integration so your users enjoy a seamless experience without leaving your application. This is popular for tenant portals and client-facing reporting platforms.
