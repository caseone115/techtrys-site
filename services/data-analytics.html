FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What cloud data platforms do you support? ▾

We have deep expertise across Snowflake, Google BigQuery, Amazon Redshift, and Azure Synapse. Our team is certified on all major cloud platforms and will recommend the best fit based on your existing infrastructure, data volume, budget, and team capability. We've delivered data warehouses on every major cloud provider for Australian property and enterprise clients.

How long does a typical data warehouse migration take? ▾

Most migrations take 8-16 weeks depending on data volume, source system complexity, and the number of consuming applications. We run a detailed discovery phase upfront to scope the work accurately and always maintain a fallback plan so your existing systems stay operational throughout the transition.

How do you handle data governance and data quality? ▾

We implement comprehensive governance frameworks including data cataloguing, automated quality monitoring, lineage tracking, and stewardship workflows. Our approach combines technology (tools like Atlan, Alation, or Collibra) with process design so governance becomes part of daily operations rather than a bottleneck.

What level of security and compliance do you build in? ▾

All our data platforms include role-based access control, encryption at rest and in transit, comprehensive audit logging, and compliance with APRA CPS 234, GDPR, and the Australian Privacy Act. We also implement data masking for sensitive fields and can support industry-specific frameworks such as ISM and SOC 2.

What's the typical engagement timeline and delivery approach? ▾

We start with a 2-week discovery and assessment phase, followed by iterative delivery in 2-week sprints. Most clients see their first data flowing within 4-6 weeks. We use agile methodology with fortnightly stakeholder demos, transparent backlogs, and clearly defined acceptance criteria for every increment.
