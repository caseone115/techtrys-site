FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a fractional CDO and when do I need one? ▾

A fractional CDO is a part-time chief data officer who provides strategic data leadership without the cost of a full-time executive hire. You need one when your data initiatives are stalling due to lack of strategic direction, your leadership team needs data literacy and guidance, or you're preparing for a major transformation but aren't ready for a permanent CDO.

How long does a typical consulting engagement run? ▾

Our strategy sprints are 2-week focused engagements. Fractional CDO placements typically run 6-18 months with 2-3 days per week of executive time. Transformation retainers are 12-month commitments with a dedicated advisory team. We're flexible — some clients start with a sprint, then transition into fractional support as their needs evolve.

What size team will be assigned to our engagement? ▾

Strategy sprints are led by a single senior consultant with access to our broader team for specific expertise. Fractional CDO engagements give you a dedicated executive plus ad-hoc access to our practice leads. Transformation retainers include a core team of 2-3 consultants plus specialists as needed. We right-size every engagement to maximise value without overhead.

What kind of outcomes should I expect from a fractional CDO? ▾

Typical outcomes within the first 3-6 months include a validated data strategy and investment roadmap, established data governance frameworks, improved data literacy across the leadership team, vendor evaluations and selections, and a clear execution plan with prioritised initiatives. Longer-term, you'll see measurable improvements in data quality, reporting velocity, and business value from data investments.

How do you handle confidentiality and IP protection? ▾

All engagements begin with a mutual non-disclosure agreement. We treat your data and business context as strictly confidential. Our team members sign individual confidentiality agreements, and we use enterprise-grade data handling practices with encrypted communications. Your strategy, roadmaps, and intellectual property remain yours — we retain only our methodology frameworks.
