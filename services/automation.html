FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between RPA and AI automation? ▾

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) excels at rule-based, repetitive tasks like data entry and reconciliation. AI automation handles unstructured scenarios — reading documents, understanding context, and making decisions. We combine both approaches: RPA for the structured steps and AI for the judgement calls, delivering end-to-end intelligent automation.

How quickly will I see ROI from automation? ▾

Most clients see positive ROI within 3-6 months. Simple, high-volume automations (like data reconciliation or invoice processing) can break even in as little as 8 weeks. Our process automation assessment includes a detailed ROI calculator that shows expected payback periods for each candidate process before you invest a dollar.

How do you decide which processes to automate first? ▾

We use a structured prioritisation framework that scores processes on volume, frequency, rule-consistency, manual effort, and error rate. The highest-scoring processes — typically invoice processing, lease administration, report generation, and data reconciliation — deliver the fastest wins and build momentum for broader automation programs.

Will automation integrate with our current systems? ▾

Yes — we build automation that connects with your existing CRM, ERP, property management, and accounting systems through APIs, database connectors, or UI automation where necessary. Our team has extensive experience integrating with systems like SAP, MRI Software, Yardi, Salesforce, and Xero without disrupting existing operations.

How do you scale automation across the organisation? ▾

We establish a Centre of Excellence (CoE) with governance frameworks, bot monitoring dashboards, change management processes, and reusable automation components. This lets you scale from pilot to enterprise-wide deployment without chaos. We also train your team to build and maintain automations in-house after we exit.
