About Techtrys Consulting

We're a team of data strategists, engineers, and AI specialists on a mission to make enterprise data work smarter — not harder.

Our Story

Built by Practitioners, for Enterprise

Techtrys Consulting was founded in 2018 by a group of data practitioners who saw the gap between what enterprise data teams could do and what they were actually achieving.

Having led data transformations at companies like Mirvac, Stockland, and Westpac, our founders recognised that the property and enterprise sectors were underserved by traditional consulting firms that delivered strategy without execution — tools without outcomes.

Today, we're a team of 40+ data scientists, engineers, BI developers, and automation specialists serving over 40 enterprise clients across Australia. We maintain a 98% client satisfaction rate and have never lost a client to a competitor.

Our philosophy is simple: measure everything, automate what you can, and never stop iterating.

Our Journey

Milestones

2018

Expanded to Melbourne

Opened our Melbourne office and launched the Power BI practice in response to growing demand for enterprise reporting solutions.

20 Clients Strong
